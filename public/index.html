About KRRC

The Kashmir Research & Resource Center (KRRC) is a digital archive dedicated to preserving and making accessible the rich scholarly heritage of Kashmir.

Our mission is to collect, digitize, and provide open access to books, research articles, manuscripts, and historical documents related to Kashmir in all its dimensions - history, culture, politics, geography, language, literature, religion, art, and more.

Features

  • AI-powered full-text search across all documents
  • AI chatbot to explore and ask questions about the archive
  • Multi-language support (Kashmiri, Urdu, Hindi, Persian, English, and more)
  • Community uploads with admin review
  • Reference-only access (no downloads) to protect copyright
  • Rich metadata and categorization for every document

Languages

The archive supports documents in English, Kashmiri, Urdu, Hindi, Persian, Arabic, Punjabi, Sanskrit, Dogri, Balti, Shina, and more South Asian languages.
